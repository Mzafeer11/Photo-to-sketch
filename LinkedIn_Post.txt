✏️ Unpaired Image Translation with CycleGAN

How do you teach a machine to draw sketches without having a paired dataset for every photo? Enter CycleGAN.

My latest project uses Cycle-Consistency Loss to learn a mapping between photos and hand-drawn sketches. It's a robust solution for style transfer tasks where paired data is hard to come by.

Explore the modular implementation: https://github.com/Mzafeer11/Photo-to-sketch
